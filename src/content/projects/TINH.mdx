What It Is and My Team

This Is Not Home is the graduation project I am developing alongside my teammates, Sebastian Contreras and Martina Olavarrieta.

## What It's About

TINH (This Is Not Home) is a cold survival experience that explores the reality of an animal more intelligent than the rest, forced to adapt to new climates, unfamiliar terrain, unknown wildlife, and an entirely different food chain within a habitat it does not recognize as home.

Through its cold color palette, nostalgic yet inspiring music, and the constant danger lurking around the player, the experience encourages players to put themselves in the protagonist's place and fight to survive at all costs.

TINH will be developed in a semi-realistic 2D pixel art style, showcasing art inspired by Antarctica while evoking a sense of comfort and nostalgia in the player.

## Planning

Our goal is to complete the first playable versions by the end of 2026 and release the game in Early Access on Steam. After that, we plan to continue supporting and improving the project over time until we are fully satisfied with the experience we have created.
